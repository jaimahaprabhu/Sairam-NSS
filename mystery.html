MYSTERY

To cheer up the participants and to know their knowledge in NSS activities and activists this mystery game is organised.

*Round 1*:Connections
*Round 2*: Hidden Figure
*Round 3*: One clue - One word

RULES

  • Maximum three and minimum two members can form a team.
  • Questions will be based on social activities, environment and NSS.

    • Connections

  • Time alloted is one minute for each question.

    • Hidden Figure

  • Time alloted is 1.5 minutes.
  • Three maximum clues will be given and points will be reduced for each clue.

    • One clue - One word

  • Time alloted is 2 minutes.
  • Three maximum clues and points will be reduced for each clue.
